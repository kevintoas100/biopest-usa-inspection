Standards & Compliance Context

Why this matters for a food-production facility in Illinois

  • Illinois Structural Pest Control Act (IDPH): commercial applications must be performed by licensed/certified technicians with proper records and service documentation.
  • FDA Food Code & FSMA preventive controls: an active pest management program with monitoring and corrective actions is expected; pest activity in production areas is a critical violation.
  • Third-party audit readiness (SQF / BRCGS / AIB): require a device map, trend analysis, service logs, corrective-action tracking and an IPM plan — commonly the biggest gaps we find.
  • EPA label & recordkeeping: products must be labeled, stored correctly, applied per label, with SDS and application records on site.
